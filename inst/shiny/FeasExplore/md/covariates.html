This section shows the frequency of covariates recorded for patients in a given cohort before (between day -730 and day -1 inclusive) and after (between day 0 and day 30 inclusive) the index date. Covariates are classified into a domain according to origin of the information in the Common Data Model:

  • condition: The frequency of active condition eras recorded in the condition_era table, aggregated according to the SNOMED heirarchy.
  • drug: The frequency of active drug eras recorded in the drug_era table, aggregated to the ingredient and according to the ATC classificaiton heirarchy.
  • procedure: The frequency of procedures recorded in the procedure_occurrence table.
  • measurement: The frequency of measurements recorded in the measurement table.
  • meas value: The frequency of measurements with a corresponding unit and value recorded in the measurement table.
  • observation: The frequency of observations recorded in the observation table.
